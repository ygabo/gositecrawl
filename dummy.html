POT: Probability of Touch

Tasty Bites

Wed | LIVE 8:00-8:20am/CT

  • Description
  • Keywords

We've seen some confusion surrounding some of our tastytrade acronyms. Specifically POP, POS and POT. POP (prob. of profit) and POS (prob. of success) both refer to the probability that any given strategy makes money (at least $0.01). Today we're making sense of Probability of Touch (POT). Probability of Touch (POT) predicts how often a stock will touch a strike between now and the expiration date. It is reasonable to assume that at some point, the short strikes of our option strategies will be tested. As such, probability of touch helps us in position sizing and managing risk on order entry. In today's segment we show how to estimate POT and we test our accuracy with a study using SPX, AAPL, and AMZN.
